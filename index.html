Charity Website project

Designed as a testament to my advanced understanding of WordPress, this project involved creating a revitalized web presence for WoodGreen Pet Charity, an organization I've had the honor of volunteering for. Harnessing the power of WordPress, a "Blocksy" child theme was crafted and the "Charity" template was employed to shape the website's architecture. PHP played a pivotal role in creating the enqueue function, seamlessly integrating custom CSS, JavaScript, and PHP. A significant accomplishment within this project was the inclusion of cutting-edge AI technology. Using DALL-E, I generated authentic images, such as the poignant depiction of a homeless cat for the Hero section. Meanwhile, OpenAI's GPT-3.5 was instrumental in generating engaging articles for the News section. One of the standout challenges was customizing the footer on every page, requiring the creation of a dedicated PHP function. This project not only underscores my ability to navigate complex technical hurdles but also my innovative use of AI in web development.
